Tips for printing

  • Size: Print the QR at least 3×3 cm so phone cameras can focus easily.
  • Material: Matte laminate scans better than glossy. Ask your 인쇄소 for "무광 코팅".
  • Placement: Eye level on the table, not flat against a window or under glass.
  • Testing: After printing, scan with both an iPhone and an Android before placing on tables.
